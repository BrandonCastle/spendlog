# User Guide

## Accessing the app

Open a browser and go to http://your-pi-ip/spendlog/

## Adding an expense

1. Click + Add Expense
2. Fill in the amount, description, category, and date
3. Click Save

## Editing an expense

1. Click Edit next to the expense you want to change
2. Update the fields
3. Click Update

## Deleting an expense

1. Click Delete next to the expense
2. Confirm the deletion

## Viewing the summary

Click Summary in the nav to see total spending per category.

## Viewing team members

Click Members in the nav to see the project team.
